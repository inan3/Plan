Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Plan mobile application ("Application") provided by Inan Ilik Ilik ("Service Provider") as a freemium service. The Application is offered “AS IS”.

Information Collected and Use

When you install and use the Application, we may collect:

  • Technical data: IP address, device type and operating system, app version, access date and time, pages visited and time spent in the App.
  • Approximate location (with your prior permission) to deliver location‑based content and analytics.
  • Optional identifying data you provide such as name, email, age, phone number and profile image.

We use this data to operate, maintain and improve the Application, personalise your experience, send important notices or marketing (with consent), ensure security and comply with legal obligations.

1. Data Controller and Contact

Name: Inan Ilik Ilik
Email: soporte@plansocialapp.es
Address: Spain.

2. Personal Data Collected

Identifying data (name, email, age, phone, image), technical data, approximate location and usage data.

3. Purpose of Processing

Provide and improve the service, personalise content, analyse usage, manage your account, send notifications (with consent) and guarantee security and legal compliance.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Consent, contract performance, legal obligation and the Service Provider’s legitimate interest.

5. Third‑Party Services

The Application integrates these third‑party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  1. Google Play Services
  2. Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Realtime Database, Storage, Cloud Functions, Analytics & Messaging)
  3. Google Sign‑In
  4. Google Maps Platform (Places / Geocoding API) & Geolocator
  5. Google Fonts
  6. Hostinger (SMTP email delivery)
  7. Firebase Hosting

6. Data Disclosure and Access

We never sell your data. We may share it with contracted providers, competent authorities or other users (when you publish content). Technical or location data is shared only in aggregated or anonymised form.

7. User Rights

You can access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing, request portability and withdraw consent by emailing soporte@plansocialapp.es.

8. Data Retention

Your data is kept while your account is active and for the minimum period required to meet the purposes above and legal obligations. On request we erase data unless retention is legally required.

9. Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your account in‑app or by contacting soporte@plansocialapp.es. This removes your personal data unless legal retention applies.

10. Information Security

We apply technical and organisational measures such as encryption, access control and secure servers.

11. Use by Minors

The Application is not directed to users under 18. If a parent or guardian suspects inappropriate use or data collection, please contact us so we can remove the data.

12. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Application or its providers may use cookies or similar technologies with your consent.

13. Opt‑Out Rights

You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application using your device or app‑store procedures.

14. Changes to the Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via the Application.

15. Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.
